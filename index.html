Stop The Violence

About Us

Stop The Violence is a project based organization that is focused on the improvement of American society. We are working to build a better relationship amongst African Americans and Police Departments through the United States. This is more than just a moment, this is a MOVEMENT...

Services

Come out and join the local police forces as they offer services to the community. We are uniting the community with police officers and making a difference. Stop The Violence provides an outlet where both sides (police officers and African Americans) can come together and embrace one another.

News

Not all police officers are bad, however, it's hard to see their good behind all of the inexcusable deaths and innocent killings. Here you can find examples where police officers have done good, not only to the American community, but to the African American community.

Our Mission

Stop The Violence is a benefactor to cause that stops unnecessary killings of African Americans by the hands of police officers. This goes beyond the widespread thoughts and accusations in African American communities that police officers are not good people. The same goes for police officers, how they shouldn't stereotype young African Americans in America. Stop The Violence is a cry out to Americans that we have become complacent and blind to the issues facing a specific race, that being the African American community. Martin Luther King, Jr. once said "Our lives begin to end the day we become silent about things that matter." As Americans we have become silent. We need to make a change, but it takes more than a few people to do so. It will take an entire country and when Americans realize this, that will be the day change occurs. Stop The Violence is the first step in that process.
